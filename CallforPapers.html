Call for Papers - IEEE SCOPES 2024

The conference, organized by Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM), is a premier event focusing on the latest advancements and research in the fields of Signal Processing, Communication, Power, and Embedded Systems. Previously held at prestigious locations including IIT Delhi, IIT Kanpur, IISc Bengaluru, and others, this conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share knowledge, discuss new challenges, and explore innovative solutions in these areas. The conference provides a unique platform for networking, collaboration, and dissemination of cutting-edge research in these fields.

The conference themes, grouped in four tracks, are the following (but are not limited to)

Signal Processing Communication Power Embedded Systems
Image Processing Professional Communication Power Systems Microcontrollers
Deep Learning Wireless Communication Topics Renewable Energy IoT Systems
Machine Learning Optical Communication Smart Grids Embedded AI
